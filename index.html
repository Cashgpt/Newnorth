About Sylvan North

Sylvan North is developing biodegradable planting systems and native species propagation methods to help reforestation contractors and land restoration partners eliminate plastic waste and improve seedling survival using modular low-cost rapid reforestation pods.

What We Do

We cultivate native trees and ornamentals through tissue culture and grow them in mycelium-based, zero-plastic containers. This enables scalable planting with dramatically reduced waste, labor, and mortality.
